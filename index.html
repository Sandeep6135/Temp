Nature

Nature is the ultimate source of our living, comprising all living and non-living things. Everyone is interdependent, helping to maintain the ecosystem. Plants, animals, and humans depend on nature for survival, supplying oxygen, sunlight, soil, water, and other essential components.

Nature encompasses everything we see around us—trees, flowers, plants, animals, sky, mountains, and more. Human beings rely on nature for sustenance, including food, water, shelter, and clothing. The vibrant colors in nature contribute to the beauty of our Earth.

Hacker

The term “hacker” originally referred to skilled programmers proficient in machine code and operating systems. These individuals excelled in problem-solving and often interpreted competitors' code for small software companies.

Today, the term has taken on a negative connotation. Ethical hacking involves authorized access to systems, typically under a contractual agreement.

Pollution

Pollution is a term familiar to many. It signifies the presence of unwanted substances in our environment, often resulting from human activities. The urgent need to address pollution arises from its detrimental effects on the planet.

Pollution impacts quality of life in ways that may not be immediately visible, such as air quality affected by natural gases and carbon dioxide emissions, which contribute to global warming.

Super Computer

India is making significant strides in supercomputing, now ranked fourth globally, following the US, China, and Germany.

Ancient Civilization

Ancient civilizations encompass a range of complex societies that developed advanced social structures, technologies, and cultural practices in the past. Some of the most well-known include Mesopotamia, Egypt, the Indus Valley, China, Greece, Rome, and the Maya. These civilizations, among others, laid the foundations for many aspects of modern society, including governance, agriculture, mathematics, and art.

Ancient civilizations laid the groundwork for modern society through significant advancements in various fields.
